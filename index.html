ABOUT

Web Developer | Mountain Enthusiast | Involved Citizen


I am a project oriented person who enjoys challenges. In the last two years I started programming and found it suits my style of continual learning and ability to touch upon many different sectors. I have successfully launched 5 websites, worked remotely on Hackathons, and completed my first year of a Computing Science Degree online.

I am currently completing a Web Development Bootcamp with Brainstation in Vancouver, BC.

My journey towards Web Development began seven years ago. I knew I needed a change and I was keen to study Geographic Information Systems. Yet, my plans didn’t move in a linear direction; I sold a business and had two more children. As I was waiting to start the GIS program a friend suggested I build a project in GIS. While building my first project VTT Serre Che, I discovered computer programming. I enjoyed how I was able to create and build my ideas with code.

VTT Serre Che later transformed into Bike Briançon. After I created Bike Briançon, I started Briançon Fantomes. At the time I was living in France and was passionate about the medieval town I was living in and created an Audio Theater Tour Briançon Fantomes.

From there I participated in Hackathons, helped friends with their websites, built websites for my family, and continued learning from online courses. This last year I finished my first year of a Computing Science Degree. It gave me a great base of understanding of how computers work, Python, Java, C, C#, and discrete mathematics.

This fall I am motivated to work within the tech industry and I am taking the Web Development Bootcamp with Brainstation so that my skills are comparable to industry standards and I will learn React, SQL, Express so I can be an efficient and valuable team member.

HACKATHONS

Eco Hackathon

Operation MotherEarth My role was mainly the project manager: I created the team via Slack group with Hackathon, created the idea, helped with story board, questions, translation, and finding the React-Native layout. The storyboard and animations were created with Figma. The application was built within 3 days using React Native. It is an educational mobile application to educate school age children about electronic waste and to inform users where and how they can recycle their electronic waste. My team was made up of myself and two Belarusians (programmer and graphic designer).
